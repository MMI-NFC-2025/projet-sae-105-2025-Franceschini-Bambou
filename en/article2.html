Biographical Book

A biographical book about a woman of science affected by the Matilda effect.

By Bambou FRANCESCHINI, published le 20/10/2025

About the author

Desanka Trbuhovic-Gjuric is a Yugoslav author. Little is known about her, except that like Mileva, she studied mathematics and physics. Proud of this double connection, she wanted through this biography to express her gratitude, for, as she wrote, « a people who do not honor their children of genius are not worthy of them. »

Book with two faces

A portrait of Mileva Marić, an unrecognized scientific genius whose contribution to Einstein's work where significant. This biography explains the life she lived with and without Albert Einstein. We can discover her secrets and the hidden aspects of her relationship with him. But this book also aims to make Mileva known to the public. Because despite everything, she deserves as much glory and a Nobel Prize as Einstein. It is a step toward the recognition she never received.
